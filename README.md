# About me
Welcome to my GitHub profile!
I am a Data Analyst and i graduated in Statistics (MSc) at University of Padova. I am very passionate about Data Science and Analytics, with particular regard to interpretable Machine Learning and complex data analysis.
It follows a brief summary of my work experience and some projects I worked on as a MSc student.

## Work experience
- *2024/02 - present* - _BI and Advanced Analytics Consultant_. My main tasks consist of data analysis and Business Intelligence, with respect to ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) data flows and DataWarehouse management. Main technical skills: SQL, SAP BW, R, Google BigQuery
- 2021/04 - 2021/07 - _Data Analyst (internship)_. During this internship, I gained experience in customer analytics, providing insights about the company's customer base through customer segmentation (clustering and RFM analysis), Market Basekt Analysis (association rules) and churn analysis.

## Projects
- *High-dimensional Data*: best project of STAT DATA CAMP, a competition organized by University of Padova.
- *Functional Data*: Analysis of Diffusion Tensor Imaging data
- *Text mining*: analysis of Tripadvisor reviews of popular Italian restaurants. Application of NLP techniques such as Latent Dirichlet Allocation to discover relevant topics. The data was scraped by means of Selenium.
- *Network analysis*: low-level implementation of the SimRank algorithm and corresponding approximations for community detection in Social Networks
