# About me
Welcome to my GitHub profile!
I am a Data Analyst and I graduated in Statistics (MSc) at University of Padova. I am very passionate about Data Science and Analytics, with particular regard to interpretable Machine Learning and complex data analysis.
It follows a brief summary of my work experience and some projects I worked on as a MSc student.

## Work experience
- *2024/02 - present* - _BI and Advanced Analytics Consultant_. My main tasks consist of data analysis and Business Intelligence, with respect to ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) data flows and DataWarehouse management. Main technical skills: SQL, SAP BW, R, Google BigQuery.
- 2021/04 - 2021/07 - _Data Analyst (internship)_. During this internship, I gained experience in customer analytics, providing insights about the company's customer base through customer segmentation (clustering and RFM analysis), Market Basekt Analysis (Apriori and association rules) and churn analysis. All the work was carried out with R, extensively using tidyverse (ggplot2, dplyr) and data visualization packages (plotly and highcharter).

## Projects
- *High-dimensional Data*: best project of STAT DATA CAMP, a competition organized by University of Padova. The project involved the analysis of a high-dimensional dataset in the field of GWAS (Genome-wide association study). With my team, I was able to provide a comprehensive analysis, using both traditional hypothesis testing (e.g. Manhattan plots) and modern Machine Learning techniques (PCA, feature extraction through ElasticNet regression and Linear Mixed Modeling), favouring interpretability over complexity.
- *Functional Data*: Analysis of Diffusion Tensor Imaging data. The project aimed at investigating the relationship between cognitive performance (measured with PASAT scores) and functional measures of fractional anisotropy. 
- *Text mining*: analysis of Tripadvisor reviews of popular Italian restaurants. Application of NLP techniques such as Latent Dirichlet Allocation to discover relevant topics. The data was scraped by means of Selenium.
- *Network analysis*: low-level implementation of the SimRank algorithm and corresponding approximations for community detection in Social Networks
